How to use Roamly
1

Enter your address

Type your Airbnb or hotel address, paste an Airbnb URL, or tap 📍 to use your current location.

2

Choose your traveller type

Solo, Couple, Family or Remote — categories and filters adapt to your trip.

3

Select categories and radius

Toggle which categories you want. Set your radius from 15 min walk to 1 hr drive.

4

Tap Roam

Pins appear on the map instantly. Larger pins with a gold ring = top rated. Tap any pin for details and a Google Maps link.

💡 Pro tip: Swipe the bottom drawer up to see a full ranked list per category.
